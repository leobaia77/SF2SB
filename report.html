Pre-Tour Sleep Debt
5.6h before D1 — the tour started in a hole
HRV was already 34.8 ms vs 59 ms baseline before Day 1 began. Sleep 5.6h before a 199 km stage reduces endurance performance 7–11% and impairs glycogen synthesis. Every downstream failure on D3 had this as a root input. The best night of the tour (D2 pre-ride: 7.3h, 120min REM) produced the best-managed stage.
Skein et al. (2011): 6h sleep reduces paced endurance performance 7%. IJSPP.

Respiratory Rate +10.8%
13.0 → 14.4 br/min sleeping — 4-night stress marker
Nocturnal respiratory rate increases as metabolic clearance load rises. The 1.4 br/min rise over 4 nights exceeds the published 1.0 br/min threshold for overreaching. Respiratory muscles themselves accumulate fatigue, impairing ventilatory efficiency during the next ride. Often ignored — almost always present in over-reached athletes.
Buchheit (2014): nocturnal resp rate increase >1 br/min over 3+ nights = overreaching marker. IJSPP.

Gran Fondo Apr 25 Protocol — 182 km / 3,105 m
  • Power targets: km 0–50 = max 130 W (IF 0.63). km 50–140 = 140–150 W (IF 0.68–0.73). km 140–182 = up to IF 0.78 by feel. Get a power meter before Apr 25 (Stages left-side for Giant TCR ~$450).
  • Carbs: 85 g/hr from H0. If temp above 22°C, dilute flasks 25% and add plain water between feeds. Solid food (rice cake or banana) at km 80–90 regardless of how you feel.
  • CGM protocol: Glance every 90 min. If SD feels volatile or you feel "off," switch immediately to diluted flask + solid food. The 90-min early warning is documented from D3 data.
  • Cadence floor: 72 rpm on all climbs. Use the 36×34 early. Low cadence compresses the gut and accelerates GI shutdown.
  • Morning HRV: Above 45 ms = race normally. 35–45 ms = conservative first 50 km. Below 35 ms = ride at IF 0.60 only — do not race. Tour started at 34.8 ms and every problem followed.
Recovery Protocol — Days 1–20 Before Gran Fondo
  • Days 1–7 (Apr 6–12): No cycling. Sleep 8–9h/night. Carbs 864g/day (8g/kg). Protein 270g/day (2.5g/kg). HRV target: above 40 ms before resuming training.
  • Days 8–14 (Apr 13–19): Z2 only. 60–90 min/session. HR cap 125 bpm. Power cap 110 W. 2–3 sessions. No climbing, no tempo.
  • Days 15–18 (Apr 20–23): One 3h Z2 flat ride + one short session with 3×10 min at IF 0.75 (~154 W). Total TSS these 4 days: under 200.
  • Apr 24: Rest. 20 min easy spin. 1,080g carbs (10g/kg). 8.5h sleep minimum. Check HRV at 06:00.
